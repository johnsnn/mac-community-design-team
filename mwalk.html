MWalk

By Tyler, Jason, Davin & Diana

About the Project

Throughout our years of living in various regions of Macau, we have noticed a recurring issue amongst elderly: Movement safety. This is why we have created MWalk to aid elderly citizens as they go about their daily activities, whether outdoors or indoors.

MWalk is an elderly friendly walking device that is convenient, versatile and safe for all users in different urban scenarios.
The walker itself is made of recyclable, sturdy and versatile materials. For our prototype, we used mostly paper and aluminium cans.
Height adjustment: all four legs’ heights can be adjusted to the user’s height and needs, with a simple pin system.
The braking system is controlled by batteries and electromagnets so it doesn’t require much manual force.
Soft material wrapped around the handles and parts of the device make it easier to grip. For our prototype, we used clay.
The wheels are stopped by the brakes powered from the electromagnets.

Introducing to seniors the innovative design: MWalk!
